Background &
My Story

I'm a B.Tech Computer Science student at IES IPS Academy, Indore, with a specialization in Data Science. My fascination with how data can uncover hidden truths about the world drives everything I build and study.

My passion lies at the intersection of data analysis, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. I believe the most powerful skill in the 21st century is the ability to ask the right questions from data — and then answer them with code.

I approach every problem analytically — decomposing complexity into structured, logical steps. Whether I'm writing SQL queries, designing Tableau dashboards, or exploring ML classification algorithms — I aim for clarity, precision, and real-world impact.

→ Currently learning: Python for Data Science, scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy, and exploring deep learning fundamentals.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing cricket, travelling to explore new cultures, or reading about the latest breakthroughs in AI research. I speak English, Hindi, and Nepali.
