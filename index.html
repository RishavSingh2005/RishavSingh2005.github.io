Human Decision Intelligence System

Decision-Centric Artificial Intelligence (HDIS)

HDIS is a research-driven AI project aimed at modeling how humans make decisions based on historical choices, behavioral patterns, and outcomes. The system focuses on learning from failures and simulating alternative decision paths to recommend better future decisions.

  • Focuses on decision behavior, not just prediction accuracy
  • Includes outcome learning and counterfactual thinking mechanisms
  • Designed as a long-term research and startup project
